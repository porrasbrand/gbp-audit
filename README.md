# GBP Audit Project

Audit and correction of Google Business Profile optimization guidelines and client reports.

## Purpose

Our Master GBP Optimization Guide and client audit reports contain outdated advice due to rapid changes in Google's features and policies throughout 2025-2026.

This project documents:
1. **What's wrong** in our existing guidelines
2. **What changed** at Google
3. **How to fix** our recommendations

## Critical Findings

| Feature | Status | Impact |

## Key Corrections

### 1. Q&A Strategy → Website FAQ Strategy
**Old:** Seed 10-15 Q&As directly on GBP
**New:** Create FAQ page on website with schema markup; Google AI pulls from there

### 2. Messaging → Alternative Channels
**Old:** Enable GBP messaging, earn "responsive" badge
**New:** Use phone, website forms, or WhatsApp integration (new 2025 feature)

### 3. Categories: 10 → 5-6 max
**Old:** Use up to 10 categories
**New:** Best practice is 5-6 maximum for better rankings

### 4. Review Responses Now Moderated
**Old:** Responses appear immediately
**New:** Google moderates responses (10 min to 30 days delay possible)
